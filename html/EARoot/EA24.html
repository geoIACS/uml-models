<br/>--<b>Name</b>--<br/>Eco Landscape Element<br/><br/>-- <b>Definition</b>--<br/>From Article 4(4)(b) of R (EU) 2021/2115, any area of the holding which is: (i) covered by landscape features subject to the retention obligation under GAEC standard 8 listed in Annex III and (ii) for the duration of the relevant commitment by the farmer, established or maintained as a result of an eco-scheme referred to in Article 31.<br/><br/>-- <b>Description </b>--<br/>It shall be a landscape feature or an area interested by an area-based eco-scheme<br/>
